# The Nullshore Expanse — One-Shot Campaign

## Core Premise

A farming village is under siege by nightmare creatures emerging from a corrupted gateway to the Nullshore Expanse. The village’s magical barrier (the **Forestall**) is believed to be failing. The adventurers are tasked with entering the Nullshore, locating the source, and sealing the breach.

Unbeknownst to the village, the forestall is not failing—it is **feeding the threat**.

---

# The Nullshore Expanse

## Description
An interstitial byway appearing as an endless sandbar between two enchanted seas:
- **The Bloodless Sea**
- **The Cerulean Sorrows**

> The sandbar is formed where the waters of the two seas meet and turn into sand.

---

## Core Rules

### Environmental Rules
- The sun is always at high noon
- Time is not naturally perceivable
- Storms are the only indicator of time progression

---

### The Two Seas
- Creatures from one sea die instantly in the other
- Ocean creatures are docile unless entered
- Entering the water triggers immediate lethal aggression

---

### Sandbar Formation
- Where the seas meet, they crystallize into sand
- The terrain subtly shifts over time

---

### Sound Deception Rule
- False houses simulate:
  - Loud waves
  - Seagulls
- Reality:
  - No birds exist
  - Water is calm near false houses

---

### Storm System
- Storms cover the entire sky
- Red and blue glow emanates from the seas
- Storm phases escalate danger and distort reality

---

### Storm Summoning
Storms can be summoned by:
- False houses
- Betrayed fishermen

---

# Interstitial Byways

## Definition
Transitional spaces between realities shaped by:
- Thought
- Terrain
- Intent
- Multiversal structure

---

## Properties
- Not designed for living beings
- Often lethal or impassable
- Exist in countless forms:
  - Crystal corridors
  - Hard-light constructs
  - Boundary zones (like Nullshore)

---

## Arcanists
- Scholars mapping byways over millennia
- Do not control byways—only survive them
- Use tools such as:
  - Resonance maps
  - Anchor runes
  - Phase compasses

---

# The Forestall

## Real World Function
- Protects the village from magical threats
- Repels hostile entities

---

## Nullshore Effect (Inversion)
- Becomes an attractor instead of a barrier
- Draws creatures and energy inward
- Feeds nearby entities

---

## Current Situation
- Villagers reinforce the forestall with ritual magic
- This energy is inverted in Nullshore
- The false house is feeding on this energy

---

# False Houses

## Nature
Predatory, semi-living structures that:
- Mimic safe dwellings
- Lure and consume prey
- Collect and integrate artifacts

---

## Behavior

### Artifact Collection
- Gather objects from across realities
- Embed them into structure as functional components

---

### Feeding
- Normally consume travelers
- This false house is externally fed by the forestall

---

### Evolution
- Has gained the ability to:
  - Summon storms
  - Build its own doorway
  - Expand into a stable byway node

---

## Structural Mechanics

Artifacts act as:
- Power nodes
- Dimensional anchors
- Structural reinforcement
