# Nullshore Expanse - Spoiler-Free Outstanding Work

This file is safe to share with a potential player collaborator. It lists design tasks that do not reveal the hidden cause of the campaign, Act 5 revelations, final encounter mechanics, or ending outcomes.

## Collaboration Rules

- Keep all work compatible with D&D Beyond.
- Use official 5e names for spells, items, monsters, and conditions when possible.
- Flag any suggested homebrew creature, NPC, item, or mechanic.
- Design toward a 4-hour session.
- Treat mysteries as mysteries. Do not define the true cause of events in this file.
